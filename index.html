Intro

My name is Hagrid. I am half man and half giant. I work as the groundskeeper for the Howarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, and I teach Care of Magical Creatures there as well. Thanks to the headmaster Albus Dumbledore, I've made Hogwarts my home, and spend my days working to care for the creatures that live at the school as well as those that live in the Forbidden Forest. It is also my job to keep an eye on the forest, and make sure that no students are venturing in unsupervised.
